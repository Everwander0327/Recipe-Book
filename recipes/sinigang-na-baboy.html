Sinigang na Baboy's Recipe

Description:

Sinigang na Baboy is a traditional Filipino sour soup known for its savory and tangy flavor, primarily derived from tamarind. It features tender pork pieces simmered with a variety of vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds pork belly or spare ribs, cut into pieces
  • 1 batch kangkong (water spinach)
  • 2 medium tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 large onion, quartered
  • 1 large radish, sliced
  • 2-3 pieces long green chili (siling haba)
  • 1 packet sinigang mix (tamarind base)
  • 6 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce (patis) to taste

Directions:

  1. In a large pot, combine pork, onion, and tomatoes. Add water and bring to a boil.
  2. Lower heat and simmer until the pork is tender, about 45 to 60 minutes.
  3. Add the radish and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the sinigang mix and fish sauce. Add the long green chilies.
  5. Add the kangkong (stems first, then leaves). Cook for 2 minutes or until the vegetables are just tender.
  6. Serve hot and Enjoy!
